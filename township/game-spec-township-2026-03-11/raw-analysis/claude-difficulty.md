## 2. Difficulty Dimensions

### a. Spatial Complexity
- **Town management**: At level 160+, the town spans a massive area with coastal city, harbor, islands (frames 20-22)
- **Barn at 12973/14020**: Near-maximum storage forces constant resource management
- **Match-3 boards**: Irregular T/L-shaped boards (frame 17) increase spatial reasoning demand
- Evidence: frames 14, 17, 20-22

### b. Temporal Pressure
- **Multiple concurrent timers**: Airport (14H 58M), Knight Pass (17D 6H), events (6D 2H, 3D 6H)
- **Production queue management**: Pastry Factory with 4 slots (frame 16) — parallelizing production
- **Event deadlines**: Mayor's Week goals (frame 12) have implicit time pressure
- **Regatta competition**: Real-time team rankings (frame 5)
- Evidence: frames 6, 9, 13, 16

### c. Cognitive Load (Late Game)
Simultaneous systems player must track:
1. Farm crop timers (planting → harvest)
2. Factory production queues (multiple factories)
3. Order fulfillment (helicopter + airport)
4. Match-3 level goals
5. Event progress (Knight Pass, Mayor's Week)
6. Team/regatta tasks
7. Card collection progress
8. Storage management (barn near full)
9. Town expansion planning

**Cognitive load rating**: Very High (8-9 concurrent systems)
- Evidence: frames 9, 12-22

### d. Motor Skill Demand
- **Town view**: Low motor demand (tap to interact, no time pressure)
- **Match-3**: Medium motor demand (move limit, not time limit — strategic, not reflex-based)
- **Overall**: Low-medium — this is a casual management game, not an action game
- Evidence: frame 17

## 4. Progression Signals

### Level Indicators
- **Player Level**: 160-161 (frames 21-22) — very deep progression
- **Star Counter**: 49 stars (frame 2), 14/100 (frame 9) — gates town expansion
- **Knight Pass Level**: 13/20 tier progress (frame 7, 15)
- **Mayor's Week**: 1200/3000 progress (frame 12)
- **Barn Capacity**: 12973/14020 (frame 14) — storage progression

### Unlock Notifications
- "Knight Pass Activated!" (frame 15) — premium tier unlocked
- Green checkmarks on completed goals (frame 12)
- "Claim" buttons lit up on reward track (frames 7, 15)

### UI Changes Between States
- New buildings appear as town grows (comparing frames 20-22 — dense city)
- Event panels stack on left side (up to 4 concurrent events)
- Product variety increases (many different food icons floating)

## 5. Difficulty Design Assessment

- **Curve Shape**: **Stepped with exponential undertone**
  - New mechanics introduce step increases (mining, airport, regattas)
  - Within each system, complexity grows gradually
  - Overall trend is exponential (8-9 concurrent systems at level 160)

- **Rest Points**: Yes
  - Card pack openings, reward claims, celebration screens
  - Production timers force natural breaks
  - No energy system for town management

- **Fail Safety**: Moderate
  - Match-3 likely has retry/booster mechanics
  - Town building has no fail state (can't lose buildings)
  - Events can be missed without penalty (but FOMO pressure)

- **Skill Ceiling**: Medium-High
  - Optimal production chain management requires planning
  - Match-3 levels have genuine puzzle difficulty
  - Competitive regatta requires strategic task selection
  - However, paying can bypass most difficulty (T-cash boosters)
